Help

Getting Started

Locate your Undertale save folder.

MAC

C:\users\[YourUsername]\Local Settings\Application Data\UNDERTALE

PC

C:\Users\[YourUsername]\AppData\Local\UNDERTALE

It should contain at least a file called undertale.ini and a file called file0, unless you haven't saved your game yet, or the save was deleted.

Loading

Once you have located your save, you can load it into the editor to edit it.

You don't have to load an existing save though, you can select a preset instead at the top, or use the default preset. Although if you aren't gonna screw around a bit with your files, why did you come here...?

Editing

Self-explanatory. Just edit the stuff you like. Check out the wiki or something if you're havin' trouble knowing what stuff is.

Saving

Finished? Simply hit the save button on the file of your choosing to download it to your computer. Easy, now just replace your original file0 and undertale.ini with the ones you downloaded.

Recovering an Erased World/Undoing Selling Your Soul

Erased your world? system_information_962 should be present in your Undertale folder.

Sold your soul to get the world back? You should also see a file called system_information_963.
